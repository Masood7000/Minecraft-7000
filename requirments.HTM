Minecraft System Requirements


MinimumRequirements


  • Operating System: Windows 7/8/10 or macOS Mojave 10.14.5 or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3210 / AMD A8-7600 APU or equivalent
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • Graphics: Integrated GPU with OpenGL 4.4 support
  • Storage: 4 GB available space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection for multiplayer

Recommended Requirements


  • Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS Mojave 10.14.5 or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 / AMD A10-7800 or equivalent
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 700 Series / AMD Radeon Rx 200 Series or equivalent with OpenGL 4.5 support
  • Storage: SSD with 8 GB available space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection for multiplayer

Note: These are general recommendations, and specific requirements may vary based on updates and modifications to the game.
